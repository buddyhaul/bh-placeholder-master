What is BuddyHaul?

BuddyHaul is an online marketplace connecting people in need of an on-demand moving and delivery service with a Buddy that has a vehicle to get the job done. BuddyHaul provides an alternative to expensive and inconvenient truck rentals and delivery/moving companies, with a convenient, affordable, and community-oriented delivery service.

We are a Canadian company currently servicing the GTA area. However, we are expanding and will be launching our services nationwide.


What do we deliver?

We deliver large, bulky items that require the use of a truck of van to transport.

Typical requests include:.

1. Furniture (couches, desks, tables etc.)

2. Apartment Moves (basement apartments, 1-2 bedroom apartments etc.)

3. Kijiji item deliveries

4. Landscaping Material Moves

5. Junk Removal and Dumping
